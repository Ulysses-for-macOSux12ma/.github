## **🔍 How to Choose the Right App for You**
- **For All-in-One Workspaces:** Notion or Craft
- **For Task Management:** Todoist, Things 3, or Microsoft To Do
- **For Writing and Notes:** Bear, Ulysses, or Obsidian
- **For Project Management:** Trello, ClickUp, or Asana
- **For Automation and Search:** Alfred
- **For Calendar and Scheduling:** Fantastical

## **💡 Pro Tips for Maximizing Productivity on macOS**
1. **Use Keyboard Shortcuts:** Learn the shortcuts for your most-used apps to speed up your workflow.
2. **Sync Across Devices:** Ensure your apps sync with iOS and other platforms for seamless access.
3. **Automate Repetitive Tasks:** Use Alfred workflows or ClickUp automation to save time.
4. **Customize Your Workspace:** Tailor apps like Notion and Obsidian to fit your unique needs.
5. **Integrate Your Tools:** Connect apps like Trello with Slack or Fantastical with Zoom for a unified workflow.

- **OS:** macOS 10.15 (Catalina) or later
- **CPU:** Intel or Apple Silicon (M1/M2)
- **RAM:** 4GB minimum (8GB recommended for heavy use)
- **Storage:** Varies by app (usually 100MB–500MB)
